The National Association of Attorneys General (NAAG) provides Tobacco Settlement Revenue data for 46 states participating in the Master Settlement Agreement (MSA) with the four largest tobacco companies in the United States.  Data are reported on an annual basis.  Four states (Florida, Minnesota, Mississippi and Texas) provide the STATE System their Tobacco Settlement Revenue data independently.
